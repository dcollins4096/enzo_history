star_maker2 (f77 routine)

Functionality

This function makes stars and adds stellar feedback to the simulation.

Inputs/Outputs

This file contains two routines, star_maker2 and star_feedback2. They are both called by Grid::StarParticleHandler.

star_maker 2 and star_feedback2 call no further subroutines.

This routine takes as inputs the standard grid information, as well as a null list of star particles.

This routine creates star particles - particles which are treated as dark matter (ie, n-body particles) for the duration of the simulation.

Notes

There are three sets of star_maker and star_feedback routines: (names). star_maker2 and star_feedback2 are the "standard" routines for AMR runs. star_maker1 and _feedback1 are experimental AMR routines, and star_maker3 and star_feedback3 are for unigrid runs, and should NEVER be used with AMR runs.
